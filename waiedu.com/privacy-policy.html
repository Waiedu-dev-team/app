Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: July 8, 2025

Privacy Policy Summary

Data Protection

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data

No Sale of Data

We never sell your personal information to third parties

Your Control

You can access, update, or delete your data anytime

Table of Contents

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, school/organization, role
  • Educational Content: Assignments, progress data, learning preferences
  • Communication: Messages through our platform, support requests

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform, time spent on activities
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type
  • Technical Data: IP address, cookies, log files (for security and performance)

2. How We Use Information

Primary Uses

Educational Services

  • • Provide personalized learning experiences
  • • Track and analyze learning progress
  • • Generate educational content recommendations

Platform Operations

  • • Maintain and improve our services
  • • Provide customer support
  • • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

We use your information solely to provide and improve our educational services. We do not use student data for advertising or commercial purposes beyond the educational context.

3. Information Sharing

We DO NOT Sell Your Data

WAIEDU never sells, rents, or trades personal information to third parties for commercial purposes.

Limited Sharing Scenarios

With Schools and Teachers

Student progress and educational data is shared with authorized educators and school administrators for educational purposes.

Service Providers

We may share data with trusted service providers who help us operate our platform (hosting, analytics, support) under strict confidentiality agreements.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to protect the safety and security of our users.

4. Data Security

Technical Safeguards

  • • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • • Secure data storage with regular backups
  • • Multi-factor authentication for accounts
  • • Regular security audits and updates

Administrative Safeguards

  • • Limited access to personal data
  • • Employee privacy training programs
  • • Data breach response procedures
  • • Regular compliance monitoring

5. Student Privacy

COPPA & FERPA Compliance

WAIEDU is designed to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA).

  • • We obtain school consent before collecting student data
  • • Parents have the right to review their child's information
  • • Student data is used only for educational purposes
  • • We provide tools for data deletion upon request

Parental Rights

Parents have the right to review, update, or request deletion of their child's personal information. Contact your school administrator or reach out to us directly.

6. Your Rights

Data Access & Control

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct information
  • Request data deletion
  • Export your data

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@waiedu.live

Response Time: Within 30 days

Verification: We may need to verify your identity
