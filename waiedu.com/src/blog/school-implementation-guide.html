Hardware Requirements

Minimum Requirements:

  • • Smartphones/tablets with camera (iOS 12+ or Android 8+)
  • • Stable Wi-Fi connection (10 Mbps minimum)
  • • 1 WAIEDU physical kit per 2-3 students
  • • Interactive whiteboard or projector

Recommended Setup:

  • • Individual tablets for each student
  • • High-speed internet (50+ Mbps)
  • • 1 WAIEDU kit per student
  • • Smart TV/display for demonstrations
