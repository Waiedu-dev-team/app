Our Story

Founded in 2021, WAIEDU emerged from a simple observation: students struggle to visualize abstract physics and mathematics concepts. Our founders, a group of AI engineers and educators, recognized the gap between traditional teaching methods and modern learning needs.

Starting in a small university lab, we developed the first prototype of our AI-powered recognition system. Today, we're proud to be at the forefront of educational technology innovation, transforming how students learn STEM subjects.
